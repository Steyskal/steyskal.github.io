UnityPlay

Web Design

Previous Next

TECHNOLOGIES:

HTML, CSS, JS(jQuery), Bootstrap, Unity, PHP, XAMPP

DESCRIPTION:

UnityPlay represents a prototype website which enables browsing articles and the users inclusion in the discussion of the news brought by the written articles. It is possible to create your own articles that are open to visitors comments. The home page shows the most recent, the most discussed and the most visited articles. Registering at the website allows you to access available games to play, rate and comment, or to upload one of your own.
